You can double check your mapping with the trainer solution.

Record customer:

/*
    copy customer information to supplier information
*/

copy THIS:id to root.supplier:id;
copy THIS.name:value to root.supplier.name:value;

Record volume:

copy THIS:\@value to root.supplier.volume:\@value;
copy THIS.height:value to root.supplier.volume.height:value;
copy THIS.weight:value to root.supplier.volume.weight:value;

Record bio:

copy THIS.height:value to root.supplier.bio.height:value;
copy THIS.weight:value to root.supplier.bio.weight:value;
