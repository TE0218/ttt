CSV Characteristics - 1

  • CSV = Character Separated Values OR Comma Separated Values
  • CSV files contain records and fields
  • Fields and records are separated by delimiter (variable length)

Default delimiter is semicolon (;)

  • Also contains any supported message type with variable length (that is not EDIFACT, ANSI X12, AECMA or TRADACOMS type)
