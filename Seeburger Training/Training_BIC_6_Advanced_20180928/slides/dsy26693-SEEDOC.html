Illustration

  1. In the destination options add a new "exchangeDestination
    NamespacePrefix" Definition

Add a new destination namespace prefix

  1. Also change the prefix for the Namespace in setxmlnamespace

tag: root
prefix: xmlns:personallink: http://example.com/namespace/for/bio

  1. Don't be afraid if you get an error when you look into this file with the MessageViewer. Use an Editor - the MessageViewer is based on the structure but we change this.

 Error: 
[Error:ID=2099;LEVEL=1] InhouseDocReader doSyntaxCheck(): offset[257]: 
the found segment 'person:height' is not in the message description.

  1. Enjoy your result in an editor

You can double check your mapping with the trainer solution.
