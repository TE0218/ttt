Usage of Error and Child mappings

Error and Child mappings are used in stanard mappings developed and maintained by SEEBURGER. For example:

  • used to check the incoming EDIFACT and ANSI messages

  • automatic generation of CONTRL/997 messages

  • main mapping with EDIFACT/ANSI envelop

  • child mappings for each EDIFACT/ANSI message

  • error mappings if the compliance check fails at the child

  • error mapping used to fill the CONTRL/997 message

  • result: positive or negative CONTRL/997
