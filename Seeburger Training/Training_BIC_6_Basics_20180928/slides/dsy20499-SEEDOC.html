EDIFACT Characteristics - 2

  • Each message (ORDERS, INVOIC, ...) starts with an UNH segment and ends with an UNT segment. The UNT segment contains the number of segments in the message.

  • Messages can be grouped by using the segments UNG and UNE.

  • The specific meaning of an element is defined by a qualifier. Example:

    • “NAD” with qualifer “BY”  = buyer address information

    • "NAD" with qualifier "SU" = supplier address information
