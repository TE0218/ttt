Date and Time Commands

Command

Example

Result

currentDate

currentDate();

"20120522"

currentTime

currentTime();

"133431"

currentTimestamp

currentTimeStamp();

"013431.921"

formatDate

formatDate("20120522", "CCYYMMDD", "DD.MM.YY");

"22.05.12"

  • It may be necessary in some cases to manipulate the values of dates. If so, there are further date commands such as addDays or getDayOfWeek.
