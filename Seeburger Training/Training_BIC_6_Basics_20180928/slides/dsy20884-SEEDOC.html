Advantages and Disadvantages of MapFiles?

Advantages

Disadvantages

  • Easy to install and to access

  • No write access

  • Very fast reading access

  • Limited protection for sensible data

Attention: Mapfiles are no longer supported for mappings with version 6.3.5 and higher. This restriction refers to the mapping version, not to the BIC runtime version. It is important to distinct between BIC runtime version and Mapping version! Mappings with a version older than 6.3.5 can run unchanged under a BIC 6.3.5 version.
