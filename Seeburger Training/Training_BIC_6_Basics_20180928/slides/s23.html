BIC MD GUI - Overview

For more details please refer to

(1) Project explorer: Administrate mapping projects and their mapping and message objects.

(2) Tool bar: Icons to operate the mapping designer: Open, import and export objects, copy&paste code, compile or test mappings etc.

(3) Source object: Shows the source structure.

(4) Destination object: Shows the destination structure.

(5) Mapping: Shows the mapping code.

(6) Warnings: Shows compiler or conversion errors.

(7) Status bar: Shows the progress of operations like exporting objects, compiling mappings etc.
