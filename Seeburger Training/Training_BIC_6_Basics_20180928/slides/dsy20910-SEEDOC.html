Why do we convert? - 2

External scenario:

  • Conversion of received data formats to the internal ERP structure and vice versa

  • Usage of stipulated data formats (e.g. BDEW defines the format to be used in the German energy sector)

  • Data volume (e.g. IDOC: An SAP-IDOC is usually about 10 times larger than the equivalent EDIFACT file)

  • Control of information

    • e.g. ensure a unique UNB reference for sent messages which may have their origin in different source systems

    • e.g. transcode the internal material identifier to the external one, used by the receiver

Internal scenario:

  • Adjustment of master data between different systems, e.g. CSV to SQL

  • Monitoring: Store process and message parameters in a database

  • Data transfer / data reconciliation of old and new systems
