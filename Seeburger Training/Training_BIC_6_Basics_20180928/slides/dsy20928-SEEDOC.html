Additional Programs - 2

  • MessageEnd

    • Executed when a message ends (UNT in Edifact, SE in ANSI X.12).

  • MessageTypeEnd

    • Executed when a message type ends (UNE in EDIFACT, GE in ANSI X.12).

  • FileEnd

    • Executed when a file ends (or in case of UNZ in EDIFACT, IEA in ANSI X.12).

  • MappingEnd

    • Executed at the physical end of the file.
