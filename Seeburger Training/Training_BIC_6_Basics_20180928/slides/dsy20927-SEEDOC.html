Additional Programs - 1

There are eight Additional Programs which may contain commands to be executed at certain steps during the conversion:

  • NewMapping

    • Executed at the physical beginning of the file. Important for settings which have to defined before actually reading the input file such as encoding parameters.

  • Root

    • Executed at the physical beginning of the file and also at every new head segment (UNB in EDIFACT, ISA in ANSI X.12).

  • NewMessageType

    • Executed if the type of message changes (UNG in EDIFACT, GS in ANSI X.12).

  • NewMessage

    • Executed when a new message starts (UNH in EDIFACT, ST in ANSI X.12).
