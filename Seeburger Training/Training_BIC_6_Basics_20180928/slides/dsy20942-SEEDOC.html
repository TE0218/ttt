Comparison BIC Converter / BIC Mapping Designer

BIC Mapping Designer (BIC MD)

  • Independently executable (without BIS)

  • Allows the definition of data structures

  • Used for creation and local testing of mappings

BIC Converter

  • Adapter as part of the BIS installation

  • Uses/Executes the mappings which have been made available via repository or have been deployed as precompiled mappings

  • Is invoked by BIS processes during a conversion step (BIC component)
