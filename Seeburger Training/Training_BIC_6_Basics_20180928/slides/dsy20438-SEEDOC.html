Loops

Command

Example

for - next

Example 1: 

for kkk% = 1 to 10
   [do something 10 times]
next
for - exitfor - next

Example 2: 

for kkk% = 1 to 10
   [do something max. 10 times]
   if kkk% > definedLimit%
      exitfor;
   endif
next
  • There is no WHILE loop. However you can use the exitfor command as shown above to simulate one.
