INHOUSE - Tutorial 2

Instruction

Illustration

  1. Import the message structure msg_Order.xml from the classmaterial.

  2. Create a new mapping (source = "PURCHASE_ORDER", destination = "Order").

Check chapter "How to Operate BIC Mapping Designer" if you need further assistance.

  1. Go to record program HEADER.

 
global positionCounter%;

//format date CCYYMMDD --> DDMMCCYY
copy format(THIS:ORDER_DATE, "CCYYMMDD", "DDMMCCYY") to 100:ORDER_DATE;

copy THIS:NAME1 to 100:ORDER_PARTY;

traceln("ORDER PARTY: " & THIS:NAME1);

copy THIS:STREET to 100:STREET;
copy THIS:CITY to 100:CITY;

  1. Go to record program POS.

 
copy THIS:POSNUM to 100.200:SEQUENTIAL_NUMBER;

//copy left 10 characters of ITEM_NUMBER
copy left(THIS:ITEM_NUMBER,10) to 100.200:ITEM_NUMBER;

copy THIS:QUANTITY to 100.200:QUANTITY;
copy THIS:PRICE to 100.200:PRICE;

//count positions
positionCounter% = positionCounter% + 1;

  1. Go to record program FOOTINFO.

 
copy THIS:ORDER_VALUE to 300:AMOUNT;

//trace position count
traceln("Counted positions: " & positionCounter%);

  1. You can compare your results with the attached trainer solution.
