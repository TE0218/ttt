BIC commands - Tutorial

Instruction

Illustration

  1. Use a NoMessage-to-NoMessage mapping. By enclosing your functions and results in a traceln-command your results will be available in the mapping log file.

  1. The formatdate command allows the formatting of date values.
 
traceln( formatDate("20120518", "CCYYMMDD", "DD.MM.YY") );
  1. The containsSubString command will check the existence of a substring in a given string value. If you need the exact position use indexOf instead.
 
traceln( containsSubstring("Item number", "num") );
traceln( indexOf("Item number", "num") );
  1. To sum up all numbers use a for loop from 5 to 20.
 
local kkk%, result%;
for kkk% = 5 to 20
    result% = result% + kkk%;
next
traceln(result%);
  1. Execute the mapping and check the logfile.
