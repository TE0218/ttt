INHOUSE Characteristics - 2

  • Fix length

  • A key field in all records to identify which information will follow

  • INHOUSE files contain records and fields

  • Standard record end is CRLF

  • The value of the field has always the same length (filled up with spaces or nulls)

  • Furthermore the fields of every INHOUSE file has continuous left-aligned or right-aligned values 

  • The fields and records are always on the same position
